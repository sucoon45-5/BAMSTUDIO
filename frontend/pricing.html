Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum booking duration?

Yes, the minimum booking is 2 hours for all studio types. This ensures enough time to set up, warm up, and get productive recordings.

Do prices include an engineer?

Yes! All studio bookings include a resident engineer at no additional cost. If you want to bring your own engineer, just let us know.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all debit/credit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payments through Paystack — our secure payment gateway.

Can I reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can reschedule from your dashboard up to 24 hours before your session. Late rescheduling may incur a fee.
