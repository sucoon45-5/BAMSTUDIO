The Story Behind BAM Studio

From a humble vision to Nigeria's most sought-after production house — BAM Studio has always been about empowering artists to create their best work.

Founded on Passion

BAM Musical & Video Studio was founded in 2016 with one mission: to give Nigerian artists access to world-class recording and production facilities without the need to travel abroad.

Over the years, we've grown from a small recording booth into a full production complex, with multiple studios, a video production wing, and a team of award-winning engineers and directors.
